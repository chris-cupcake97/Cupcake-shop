FAQs

How do i store my cakes overnight?

Please wrap the entire box in plastic wrap and keep at room temperature (Do NOT refrigerate).

How long will my cake last?

Because we bake without preservatives, we suggest enjoying your cupcakes the day they are purchased or the following day (stored overnight at room temperature).

How many people do your cakes serve?

6” round – 6-12
9” round – 12-20

Do you have nutritional information?

We have always baked entirely from scratch, using only the freshest and finest real ingredients – butter, eggs, sugar, flour, premium chocolate – which all have a higher caloric content than those made with artificial flavorings, sweeteners and preservatives (which we’d never use!). We are fully committed to not sacrificing size, flavor, or quality ingredients to simply reduce caloric count and are proud to provide only the best tasting products for your special celebrations!

Do you do custom cupcake or cake designs?

Yes, we specialize in custom hand drawn designs. Design pricing varies greatly depending upon complexity and the cake artist’s design time. Please email or call the bakery nearest you and ask to speak with a Celebration Specialist regarding a custom order. They will be happy to provide a quote.

Do you do custom shaped cakes?

Yes, we can create 2-dimensional cut-out cakes in most any shapes. We also offer 3-dimensional Cupcake Cakes, Barbie Cakes, Gift Box cakes, and Baby Block cakes.
